More about GeneVis

GeneVis is a desktop app built in 2019 by Mael Lefreuve under the direction of Fouzia Moussouni and Sandrine David-Le-Gall.
The goal was to be ablke to find co-occurences in articles belonging to the GeneView database.
The strength of this app was its ability to create a graphical visualisation of these co-occurences.
